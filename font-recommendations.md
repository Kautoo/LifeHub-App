# 🎨 Premium Font Recommendations for LifeHub

## Top Picks (Google Fonts - Free!)

### 1. **Space Grotesk** ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- Modern, geometric, techy feel
- Perfect for productivity apps
- Weights: Light, Regular, Medium, SemiBold, Bold
- Vibe: "I'm a serious but cool app"

### 2. **Plus Jakarta Sans**
- Clean, modern, slightly rounded
- Very popular in premium UI design
- Weights: ExtraLight to ExtraBold
- Vibe: "I'm friendly and approachable"

### 3. **Outfit**
- Geometric, clean, modern
- Great for headings + body
- Weights: Thin to Black (9 weights!)
- Vibe: "I'm sleek and minimal"

### 4. **Sora**
- Japanese-inspired, unique character
- Distinctive but readable
- Weights: ExtraLight to SemiBold
- Vibe: "I'm different and I know it"

### 5. **Clash Display** (from Fontshare - also free!)
- Bold, modern display font
- AMAZING for headers
- Weights: Medium to Semibold
- Vibe: "I'm premium and bold"

---

## 🔥 Font Pairing Recommendations

### Option A: Space Grotesk + Inter
- Headers: Space Grotesk Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Vibe: Modern tech

### Option B: Clash Display + Plus Jakarta Sans
- Headers: Clash Display Bold
- Body: Plus Jakarta Sans Regular
- Vibe: Premium & clean

### Option C: Outfit (all weights)
- Headers: Outfit Bold/ExtraBold
- Body: Outfit Regular/Medium
- Vibe: Consistent & sleek

### Option D: Sora + Space Grotesk
- Headers: Sora SemiBold
- Body: Space Grotesk Regular
- Vibe: Unique & distinctive

---

## 📱 Where to Use

- **Display/Headers** → Bold, ExtraBold weights (28-32sp)
- **Titles** → SemiBold (20sp)
- **Body text** → Regular/Medium (14-16sp)
- **Labels/Captions** → Medium (12sp)
- **Buttons** → SemiBold (14-16sp)

---

## 💡 Pro Tip

Don't use more than 2 font families! Pick ONE for headers, ONE for body (or same family for both).
