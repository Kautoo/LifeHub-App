# 🔤 LifeHub Font System

## ✅ Final Choice

### Headers: **Space Grotesk** ⭐
- Modern, geometric, techy feel
- Perfect for productivity apps
- Weights used: Regular (400), Medium (500), Bold (700)
- Vibe: "I'm a serious but cool app"

### Body: **Plus Jakarta Sans** ⭐
- Clean, modern, slightly rounded
- Very popular in premium UI design
- Weights used: Regular (400), Medium (500), Bold (700), ExtraBold (800)
- Vibe: "I'm friendly and approachable"

---

## 📱 Where Each Font Goes

### Space Grotesk (Headers)
- ✅ Screen titles ("✅ Tasks", "💰 Budget")
- ✅ Section headers ("To Do", "Completed")
- ✅ Big numbers (balance, counts, percentages)
- ✅ App name "LifeHub"
- ✅ Greeting text ("Good Morning ☀️")
- ✅ Stat values on cards

### Plus Jakarta Sans (Body)
- ✅ Task titles and descriptions
- ✅ Note content
- ✅ Labels and captions
- ✅ Button text
- ✅ Input field text
- ✅ Subtitles and metadata
- ✅ Empty state subtitles
- ✅ Dialog body text

---

## 🚫 What NOT to Use
- ❌ Default Android system font (Roboto)
- ❌ Generic fonts like Arial, Helvetica
- ❌ More than 2 font families in one app

---

## 💡 Pro Tip
This font pairing gives LifeHub a unique, premium feel that stands out from generic apps!
